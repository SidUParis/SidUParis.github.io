My mentoring approach emphasizes:

### üéØ **Hands-on Learning**
Students work on real research projects with immediate translational impact, gaining experience with cutting-edge computational tools and biological datasets.

### üìä **Technical Skills Development**
I provide structured training in:
- **Statistical analysis**: R programming, biostatistics, machine learning
- **Bioinformatics**: Multi-omics data integration, pathway analysis, visualization
- **Computational methods**: Algorithm implementation, reproducible research workflows
- **Clinical data analysis**: Survival analysis, biomarker evaluation, clinical trial statistics

### üî¨ **Translational Mindset**
Students learn to connect computational findings with biological mechanisms and clinical applications, understanding how their work contributes to precision oncology.

### üìù **Scientific Communication**
Regular practice in:
- Writing clear, impactful manuscripts
- Creating effective data visualizations
- Presenting at lab meetings and conferences
- Engaging with clinical and computational collaborators

## Prospective Students

### Are you interested in joining?

I welcome motivated students who are:
- Passionate about cancer research and precision medicine
- Eager to develop computational and analytical skills
- Interested in bridging biology, data science, and clinical applications
- Committed to rigorous, reproducible science

### Opportunities Available

- **PhD positions** (co-supervised with Prof. Gabriel Malouf)
- **Master's internships** (6 months, M2 level)
- **Short-term research projects** (3-6 months)
- **Collaborative projects** with your home institution

### Prerequisites

**For computational projects:**
- Basic programming experience (R or Python preferred)
- Understanding of statistics and data analysis
- Background in biology, bioinformatics, or related field
- Strong motivation to learn new techniques

**For translational projects:**
- Interest in cancer biology and clinical oncology
- Willingness to work with large datasets
- Critical thinking and problem-solving skills

### Application Process

If you're interested in working with me, please send:

1. **Brief cover letter** explaining your interests and goals
2. **CV/Resume** highlighting relevant experience
3. **Academic transcripts** (for formal positions)
4. **Research interests** - which projects excite you?

**Contact:** [lux@igbmc.fr](mailto:lux@igbmc.fr) | [xiaofan.lu@inserm.fr](mailto:xiaofan.lu@inserm.fr)
