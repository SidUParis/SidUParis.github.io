## Molecular and Translational Oncology Team

We are part of the **Department of Cancer and Functional Genomics** at the [Institut de Génétique et de Biologie Moléculaire et Cellulaire (IGBMC)](https://www.igbmc.fr/) in Illkirch, France. Our team focuses on understanding cancer heterogeneity through integrative multi-omics approaches and translating molecular discoveries into clinical applications, particularly in genitourinary cancers.

## Research Focus

Our team employs cutting-edge computational and molecular approaches to:

- **Characterize tumor heterogeneity** through multi-omics profiling (genomics, transcriptomics, epigenomics)
- **Identify predictive biomarkers** for immunotherapy and targeted therapies
- **Develop stratification tools** for personalized treatment selection
- **Understand mechanisms** of treatment resistance in kidney and urothelial cancers
- **Translate molecular discoveries** into clinical applications through prospective trials

## Collaborations

We maintain active collaborations with:

- **International research groups** in the US, China, and across Europe
- **BIONIKK trial consortium** for biomarker-driven precision oncology
- **Bioinformatics platforms** at MD Anderson Cancer Center and beyond
